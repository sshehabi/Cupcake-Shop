Little Sweetie's Cupcakery

The sweetest and smallest cupcakes in town!

Little Sweetie's Cupcakery is the local cupcake shoppe that truly prioritizes quality over quantity.

We're proud to serve the cutest cakes this side of the Pacific Ocean. Our cupcakes are made with love, magnifying glasses, and so much sugar.

Little Sweetie's Cupcakes come in the following flavors:

  • Barely There Banana
  • Little Lemon
  • Piddling Peanut Butter
  • Smallest Strawberry
  • Mini 'Mallow

Our cupcakes are so good, you'll wish they weren't so dang small!
